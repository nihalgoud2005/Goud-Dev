Gandhapu Nihal Goud
Hi, I'm Nihal — an ECE undergrad at BV Raju Institute of Technology (BVRIT), Narsapur, graduating in 2027. My coursework spans analog circuits, digital electronics, microprocessors, and communication systems.

Beyond textbooks, I'm fascinated by making hardware do things — automating homes, sensing the environment, processing signals in real time. I've worked on embedded projects using Arduino and 8051 microcontrollers, and I'm currently diving deeper into VLSI and FPGA workflows.

I'm also an active member of E-Cell BVRIT, where I sharpen skills in project management, team leadership, and entrepreneurial thinking — because good engineers also need to know how to build things people actually want.
